Web Master

Web Master Position
(Full Time, One Year Contract)

About Us:
ONPHA is a non-profit organization established to build a strong non-profit housing sector in Ontario. ONPHA represents more than 750 organizations that manage over 150,000 non-profit housing units for families, seniors, people with disabilities and the homeless. With a staff complement of approximately twenty and an annual budget of over $2 million, ONPHA is a progressive organization with a reputation for excellence.

The Opportunity:
This position is ideal for a new graduate or other individual with web expertise and hands-on experience, interested in maintaining content on a growing website in a non-profit environment. Job includes creating and updating HTML content, creating images in Adobe Photoshop, and transferring Word and Quark documents to web-friendly formats.

Requirements:
The candidate must demonstrate thorough knowledge and ability in website design and maintenance, including:
§ Demonstrated ability to create well-formed HTML code from scratch ‘by hand’ (not in “Frontpage” or “Dreamweaver”)
§ Demonstrated ability to create/edit forms
§ Some knowledge of Cascading Style Sheets
§ Demonstrated ability to perform site maintenance basics (FTP)
§ Ability to work with Photoshop to create web graphics
§ Ability to create PDFs with Adobe Acrobat
§ Proficiency in Microsoft Office
§ Experience trouble-shooting web problems

Helpful but not Required:
§ Ability to use Quark, Pagemaker
§ Working knowledge of HTML 4.01 specifications
§ Experience working with JavaScript, ASP, and VBScript
§ Knowledge of web-browser limitations and code work-arounds
§ Experience with design and layout
§ Experience creating and managing web services beyond websites (eg. Message boards, broadcast e-mail)

What we are looking for:
An organized, self-motivated, hard-working individual who pays attention to detail and is capable of working independently in a busy environment.

Salary Range:
Commensurate with experience.

Location:
Downtown Toronto
